Join the competition

The event will take place on Saturday the 7th of February 2026, on the campus of Radboud University in Nijmegen.

  • It is possible to sign up for only the workshops or only the CTF. However, we give preference to women who will be participating throughout the entire day.
  • Because the event promotes women in Cyber Security, you have to identify as a female student to participate in any of the events.
  • In total there are 52 spots available. It is possible to sign up as a PhD student. However, preference is given to highschool and university studens (MBO, HBO, WO). Sign-ups are first-come-first-serve. In case of late sign-ups, we give preference to candidates from underrepresented groups.

Sign-up!

The Capture The Flag (CTF) competition is a cybersecurity competition where participants solve cyber security related challenges to find hidden flags. The competition is open to all skill levels, but the challenges will lean more towards beginner and intermediate levels. The competition will be held on-site and will last for 3 hours.

If you do not know what a CTF is, please refer to the "What is a CTF?" page.

Sign-ups open on 7 October 2025. The sign-up form will then be accessible through the "Sign up for the Hackathon" button below. If you already wish to secure a spot, please send us an email at "hackherquest" "at" "gmail" "dot" "com".
