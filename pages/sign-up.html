The event will take place on Saturday the 7th of February 2026, on the campus of Radboud University in Nijmegen.

  • It is possible to sign up for only the workshops or only the CTF. However, we give preference to women who will be participating throughout the entire day.
  • Because the event promotes women in Cyber Security, you have to identify as a female student to participate in any of the events.
  • In total there are 52 spots available. It is possible to sign up as a PhD student. However, preference is given to highschool and university studens (MBO, HBO, WO). Sign-up in first come first serve. In case of late sign-ups, we give preference to candidates from an underrepresented group.

Sign-up deadline

Sign-ups will open in due. If you already still wish to secure to secure a spot, please e-mail us at hackherquest "at" gmail "dot" com. Please keep in mind that the schedule and workshops can change.

Hackathon (CTF)

The Capture The Flag (CTF) competition is a cybersecurity competition where participants solve cyber security related challenges to find hidden flags. The competition is open to all skill levels, but the challenges will lean more towards beginner and intermediate levels. The competition will be held on-site and will last for 3,5 hours.
