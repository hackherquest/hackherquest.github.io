$ Our Vision and Mission

We will organize a Capture The Flag competition and treasure hunt on Saturday 15th of February on the campus of Radboud University. The event is open to female high school- and HBO/WO students.

  • To bring women together in a space where they can dive into cybersecurity, learn from each other, and build confidence in their skills. Cybersecurity can feel like a tough field to break into, especially with so few women represented.
  • This event is about creating a supportive, welcoming environment where women can practice, solve challenges, and show what they're capable of.
  • The goal is to encourage more women to explore and stick with cybersecurity, build connections, and feel empowered to take on bigger roles in this space.
